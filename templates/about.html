The Future of Data Storage

DNA Vault is pioneering the next evolution in data storage. Our groundbreaking technology translates digital information into DNA sequences, offering unprecedented storage density and longevity. We're not just storing data - we're preserving humanity's digital legacy for generations to come.

Our Technology

The Problem

Traditional data storage faces critical challenges: limited capacity, high energy consumption, and hardware degradation. Data centers worldwide struggle with increasing storage demands while trying to minimize environmental impact.

Our Solution

DNA Vault's revolutionary technology converts digital data into DNA sequences, achieving unprecedented storage density. One gram of DNA can store 215 petabytes of data - equivalent to over 10,000 standard hard drives.
